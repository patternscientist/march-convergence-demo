Source arithmetic warning. The memo prints an East Coast subtotal of 812 buses and a national total of 1,004, but its listed East Coast entries sum to 792 and all listed bus entries sum to 984. The flow map therefore animates 984 itemized buses; printed totals remain visible as source-reported values.

Read this as a planning snapshot, not attendance. The memo excludes cars, regularly scheduled carriers, and unreported groups; southern coaches from Birmingham, Miami, and Jacksonville were confirmed without passenger counts; New York figures “barely touch” Brooklyn, Harlem, and the Bronx. Final crowd estimates were roughly a quarter million.
